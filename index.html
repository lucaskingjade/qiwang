About Me

Get to know my background and research journey

I'm a researcher with a passion for pushing the boundaries of what's possible with generative AI and computer vision. My work spans from fundamental research in neural networks to practical applications that create real-world impact.

Currently, I'm working at Alibaba Tongyi Lab where I focus on developing next-generation generative models for multimedia content creation and understanding. My research interests include diffusion models, large language models for vision, and neural rendering techniques.

Prior to joining Alibaba, I pursued my academic journey at CentraleSupélec, where I developed a strong foundation in machine learning, computer vision, and optimization.
